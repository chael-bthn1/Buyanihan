About Buyanihan

Buyanihan connects verified local MSMEs with nearby consumers through a safe, simple, and mobile-friendly marketplace inspired by Filipino community values. We help local sellers grow their business while giving consumers access to quality local products.

Our mission is to promote the Filipino spirit of bayanihan by supporting local entrepreneurship and creating a sustainable community-driven marketplace.
